## 原文 (日本語)

### 第十条第一項

<!-- segment: hashira id: kokuzei-tsuusoku-hou-shikkourei-art-10-p1-hashira -->

<!-- segment: hashira id: kokuzei-tsuusoku-hou-shikkourei-art-10-p2-hashira -->
